Lecture Schedule (Tentative)

  2. Week 3 to 5/6: Hardware design of AES

  3. Week 7 to 9: Side-channel attacks

    • Power attack basics

    • Power attack countermeasures

  4. Week 10 onwards

    • Fault attacks
    • Fault attack countermeasures
    • Advanced topics
    • Paper presentations
